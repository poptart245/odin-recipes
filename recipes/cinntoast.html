Cinnamon Toast

Description

Cinnamon toast is one of the easiest recipes out there, but I always find it so satisfying. It brings back memories of summertime mornings watching tv with my sisters and eating our way through the pantry out of pure boredom. We would make cinnamon toast often because it was so simple we didn't have to ask mom to help us.

The warmth of the melted butter mixed with the sweetness of the sugar and the earthiness of the cinnamon powder create a delicate flavor that is often overlooked in store-bought cinnamon rolls and similar such items, and for me that is what gives the nostalgia that extra kick in this quick breakfast dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices bread (your choice of type; I like split top honey wheat)
  • 2 teaspoons butter
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar

Steps

  1. Put slices of bread into the toaster on the medium-high setting (depending on your toaster) and toast
  2. Remove toast from toaster and butter one side
  3. Let butter melt slightly, then sprinkle liberally with sugar and cinnamon powder.
  4. Once cool, enjoy with a glass of milk or fruit juice.
